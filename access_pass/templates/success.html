DATACENTER (DC) RULES

Any person authorized to enter the DC must adhere to the following rules:

  1. Shall NOT, without the prior written consent of KRA, divulge any information pertaining the data centre to any person except their own employees who need to know the same. Such persons shall also be bound by the KRA DC Non Dis-closure Agreement.
  2. Unauthorized devices and equipment not declared in Section A above ARE NOT allowed in the Data Centre.
  3. Photography IS NOT allowed within and outside the Data Centre Facilities.
  4. Food and drinks ARE NOT allowed within the Data Centre Facilities.

The KRA Data Centre facility is classified as a “Restricted Area” and access to it is therefore subject to Information System Security and Corporate Security policies and regulations. The Head of ICT Division and Chief Manager ITI reserve the right of admission.
