DC Rules

Anyone seeking access to the DC must adhere to the rules below for harmonious coexistence and operation of the services and equipment in the facility;

  1. Do not enter the DC with shoes-use designated footwear-DC is a dust free zone
  2. Do not eat inside the DC
  3. Bags are not allowed inside the DC
  4. Always register entry and exit from the DC-Manually or/and electronically.
  5. Access to DC is only for authorized service providers, contractors and staff.
  6. Access to datacenter shouid be sought at-least one daybefore.
  7. Always ensure the doors to the DC are closed.
  8. In case of fire/alarm exit through the designated fire exits & notifi, the security guard and the DC staff.
  9. Always maintain the set standard (NDC is a tier III DC) when undertaking any activity in the DC. E.g all network & power cables and devices should be properly managed and labeled.
  10. Always follow instructions/designs provided by your supervisor.
  11. Always seek guidance from DC staff if you are not sure what you should do.
  12. A11 equipment going in and out the datacenter must be recorded on the provided counter book for traceability and accountability.
  13. Everyone entering to the DC must provide identification documents i.e. ID/Passport.
  14. Removable storage media (Memory cards, flash disk, HDD) are not allowed into the DC.
