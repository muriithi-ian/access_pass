DC Rules

Anyone seeking access to the DC must adhere to the rules below for harmonious co-existence and operation of the services and equipment in the facility;

  1. Access to the Data Centre should be sought at-least ONE DAY before.
  2. Always REGISTER entry and exit from the DC manually or/and electronically.
  3. Everyone entering the DC must provide IDENTIFICATION DOCUMENTS, i.e. ID/ Passport.
  4. Access to the DC is only for AUTHORIZED service providers, contractors and staff.
  5. Always follow instructions/designs provided by the officer-in-charge.
  6. All equipment going in and out of the Data Centre must be RECORDED on the provided counter book for traceability and accountability.
  7. Removable storage media (Memory cards, flash disk, HDD) are NOT ALLOWED into the DC.
  8. Bags are NOT allowed inside the DC
  9. DO NOT enter the DC with shoes. Use designated footwear. The DC is a dust free zone.
  10. Always ensure the doors to the DC are CLOSED (Both entry and exit).
  11. Always maintain the SET STANDARD (NDC IS A TIER III DC) when undertaking any activity in the DC. E.g all network & power cables and devices should be properly MANAGED AND LABELED.
  12. In case of a fire/an alarm, exit through the designated fire exits & notify the security guard and the DC staff
  13. DO NOT EAT inside the DC.
  14. Always seek guidance from the officer-in-charge if you are not sure what you should do.
